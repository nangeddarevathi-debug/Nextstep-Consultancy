About Us

Empowering engineering students to achieve their career dreams

Our Story

NextStep Engineer was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to guide engineering students through the complex landscape of career choices after B.Tech. What started as a small initiative to help a few students has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of engineering graduates.

Our founders, themselves engineering graduates, experienced the confusion and lack of guidance that many students face. They realized that with the right support, every engineering student could achieve their career goals, whether it's pursuing higher studies, landing dream jobs, starting a startup, or exploring other paths.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 10,000 students navigate their career journeys, with a success rate of 95% and thousands of successful placements and admissions.
